About CustomFurnish

CustomFurnish specializes in providing end to end interior solutions for homes & offices. CustomFurnish has over years built up a strong design expertise backed by state-of-the-art manufacturing. Additionally, CustomFurnish reaches to the end customer directly cutting out all middle men and has been able to provide best value for money.
